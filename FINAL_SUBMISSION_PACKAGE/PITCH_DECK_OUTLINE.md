# Final Pitch Deck Outline

## Slide 1: Problem
- Income instability for delivery workers during disruptions.
- Delayed/manual insurance claims cause trust and liquidity issues.

## Slide 2: Solution
- AI-powered parametric insurance platform (Devtrails).
- Automatic disruption detection, claim scoring, and payout simulation.

## Slide 3: Worker Persona
- Typical gig worker profile.
- Weekly risk profile and income exposure.

## Slide 4: Product Workflow
- Login -> Risk scoring -> Weekly premium -> Trigger monitoring -> Auto claim -> Fraud checks -> Payout.

## Slide 5: AI + Fraud Architecture
- ML microservice for risk_score.
- Fraud service with behavioral and historical anomaly checks.
- Trigger engine and payout pipeline.

## Slide 6: Pricing and Unit Economics
- Weekly premium model.
- Simulated loss ratio overview.
- Worker affordability and insurer sustainability assumptions.

## Slide 7: Phase 3 Outcomes
- Advanced fraud detection (delivery context)
- Instant payout simulation
- Worker + insurer intelligent dashboards

## Slide 8: Demo Evidence
- Link to 5-minute video.
- Key screenshots: dashboard, claims, payout, insurer analytics.

## Slide 9: Go-to-Market
- Pilot partnerships with gig platforms/cooperatives.
- City-wise rollout strategy.

## Slide 10: Ask
- Pilot support, insurer partnerships, and platform credits.
